Setting the Record Straight

What Tarot Is & Is Not

Tarot Is ✦
  • ✦A sacred tool for self-reflection and inner clarity
  • ✦A compassionate mirror for your own deep wisdom
  • ✦Guidance for navigating decisions and life transitions
  • ✦A space for honest emotional processing and exploration
  • ✦An illumination of current energies and possibilities
  • ✦Empowering — your free will is always paramount
Tarot Is Not
  • ○A prediction of a fixed, unchangeable future
  • ○A replacement for professional medical or legal advice
  • ○A magic solution to life's genuine challenges
  • ○Something designed to create fear or dependency
  • ○Connected to dark forces or superstition
  • ○Something requiring any prior experience
